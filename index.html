The Challenge

Why We Need TRUEVNDR

The Vendor Struggle

Today, millions of local vendors in India — including clothing stores, cafes, salons, bakeries, tattoo studios, boutiques, and lifestyle businesses — struggle with digital visibility. Most small businesses rely only on Instagram pages, word-of-mouth marketing, or expensive marketplace platforms that are not designed for local discovery.

The User Need

At the same time, users are actively looking for local fashion finds, trending cafes, hidden gems, nearby services, and unique stores in their cities. However, there is currently no dedicated platform focused purely on discovering local businesses in a visual, social, and city-first way.

The Ecosystem

How TrueVndr Solves This

TrueVndr creates a discovery ecosystem where vendors can showcase their products, services, food, and brand identity through visual posts, while users can explore businesses in categories like Fashion, Food, and Lifestyle & Services.
